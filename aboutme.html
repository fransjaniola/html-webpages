About Me

My Journey

Hello! I'm Frans Janiola, an SEO Specialist and Full Stack Web Developer based in the Philippines.

My journey into the digital world began with a deep fascination for how search engines operate. This curiosity led me to master SEO, focusing on strategic keyword research, on-page optimization, and technical SEO.

Realizing the importance of a comprehensive approach, I expanded my expertise to full stack web development. I honed my skills in front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Angular, and back-end technologies such as Node.js, Python, PHP, and Ruby on Rails.

This blend of SEO and web development allows me to create optimized, high-performing websites that provide an exceptional user experience.
